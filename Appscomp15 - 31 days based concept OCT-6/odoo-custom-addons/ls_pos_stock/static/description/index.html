POS Stock

User display product stock and Hide out of stock products

Key Highlights

POS Stock module allows you to display product stock at point of sale.
Don't allow customers to order out of stock products .
  • Overview
  • Features
  • Screenshots

Overview

Odoo POS Stock module allows you to display product stock .
Also, you can hide all out of stock products.

Features

This module allows you to display product stock at point of sale based on the configuration.
Also, customers can't purchase out of stock products.

Screenshots

Enable 'Display stock in POS' to display product's stock .
Now, you will see all product's stock at point of sale.
Enable 'Hide all out of stock products'to hide all out of stock products.
Here, all products which are out of stock will be hidden.
