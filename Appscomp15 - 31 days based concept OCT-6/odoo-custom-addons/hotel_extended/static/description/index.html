Hotel Managment

Most Advanced Open-Source Hotel Mangement Software

Overview

Hotel management is critical to the success of a guest accommodation business, and the management and finance department oversees the core day-to-day operations. This can include managing hotel services, Room Reservations, and meeting with business partners.

Key Features

Provides Hotel Reservation Approval process in real-time informations.

Hotel Rooms types of Beds availability in the specific rooms and amenities of each room

New Guest Registration form contains basic info of the guest like, Name,Address & Identity Attachments.

Hotel Reservation Dashboard will show you the room availability based on the Date Range selected.
